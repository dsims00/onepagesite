Daniel Simakovsky


Front End Developer

Hi, I'm Dan. I recently started learning to code. I am seeking opportunities to learn and develop the skills to get a job as a front end developer.

A bit about myself: I've been in the food/restaurant biz for over 10 years, working at some of the best restaurants in NYC, including Gordon Ramsay at the London and One if by Land, Two if by Sea. Currently, I manage food service operations at Mount Sinai Hospital.


What I realize now, what truly drives me is producing results that I can see. I believe that's what led me to cooking in the first place and also, what draws me to coding. I love creating solutions to problems, building things and learning.
